Products

Shanghai Saco Textile develops and manufactures finished garments across five core categories — from sampling and development to full industrial-scale production.

Socks & Hosiery

Industrial socks and hosiery programs with controlled fit and stable production standards. From everyday fashion to trekking and high-performance sports.

Materials & Compositions

  • Organic cotton
  • SoftSpin™ cotton
  • Recycled nylon
  • Merino wool
  • Recycled acrylic
  • Plant-dyed with IndiDye®
  • Polylana®
  • Eco viscose
40M pairs / year

Seamless

Seamless garments engineered for scalable production, comfort and technical precision. Innerwear and outerwear applications across activewear, base layers and underwear.

Materials & Compositions

  • Recycled polyamide
  • Organic cotton
  • Recycled polyester
  • Silk
  • Wool
  • SoftSpin™ cotton
Men & Women programs available

Underwear

Next-to-skin programs developed for brands seeking reliable production and controlled performance. From sampling to industrial scale.

Materials & Compositions

  • Organic cotton
  • Recycled nylon
  • Eco viscose
  • Veglon® bio based
  • RWS Responsible Wool
10M pieces / year

Swimwear

Swimwear programs combining trend-driven design with scalable production. Bikinis, one-pieces and beachwear developed from our Barcelona design studio.

Materials & Compositions

  • Recycled polyamide
  • Recycled polyester
  • Econyl® regenerated nylon
  • Lycra® elastane
Bikinis one-pieces & beachwear

Babywear & Kids

Babywear combining sewn garments and fully fashioned knitwear. Soft, safe materials for infant and kids programs in organic and sustainable fibres.

Materials & Compositions

  • Organic cotton
  • CMIA Cotton Made in Africa
  • Recycled cotton
  • SoftSpin™ cotton
  • RWS wool
3M pieces / year

Silk Garments
