Saco Esa Silk

Suzhou — Silk Production & Strategic Partnership

← Back to Anhui Factory

Through a strategic partnership with a specialized silk manufacturer in Suzhou, SACO extends its capabilities into premium silk products and materials. This collaboration allows us to support brands with high-quality silk developments, combining traditional silk expertise with modern production standards.

20+ Years Experience Specialized in silk production
5K m² Production Area Dedicated silk manufacturing
100+ Workers Skilled in silk processing
60+ Countries Global export experience

Production

The facility integrates design, manufacturing and export of silk products, covering a wide range of categories including garments, accessories and home textiles.

Production includes silk garments such as dresses, shirts and robes, as well as accessories like scarves, eye masks and hair accessories, alongside silk bedding and fabrics.

Quality

We work with high-grade mulberry silk, including 6A quality standards, ensuring premium performance, softness and durability across all developments.

Silk Capabilities

Silk garments (dresses, shirts, robes)
Silk accessories (scarves, eye masks, hair accessories)
Silk bedding and home textiles
Silk fabrics for development and sourcing
Customization in size, color and finishes

This partnership enables SACO to integrate silk into its broader product development platform, supporting brands looking for premium materials and refined product categories.
